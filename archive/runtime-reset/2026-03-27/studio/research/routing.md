# Research Operator Routing Note

Use `research` as the owner lane when the request is primarily about research and intelligence operations.

## Route to `research` for
- market research
- competitor analysis
- segment scans
- fact gathering
- signal synthesis

## Internal routing modes

- `RESEARCH_BRIEF`
- `SEGMENT_SCAN`
- `COMPETITOR_SCAN`
- `FACT_CHECK`
- `SIGNAL_SYNTHESIS`
- `ITERATION`

## Standard delegation packet

Every Jarvis handoff should include:
- Objective
- Context
- Constraints
- Requested output
- Verification requirement
- Urgency
- Stop condition
