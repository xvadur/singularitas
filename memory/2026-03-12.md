# 2026-03-12

## Xvadur positioning and operating model

- Xvadur strategy shifted from a clinic/dental-first wedge to a broader voice-agent offer for businesses that miss calls, lose leads, and fail at follow-up.
- The desired operating model is a team of narrow agents, not one mega-agent.
- The intended runtime should be a supported persistent surface such as Discord or Telegram rather than webchat.

## Google workspace migration

- Daily memory should live in `memory/YYYY-MM-DD.md`.
- Migration direction is to keep the current skill UX but move backend execution toward `gws` instead of `gog`.
- `gws` was verified as usable for core work:
  - Gmail works
  - Calendar works
  - Drive works
  - Sheets surface/schema is available
- Not every Google surface is fully ready yet; for example, some APIs like People may still need enablement/scopes.

## Account separation

- Personal Google auth should stay isolated in `~/.config/gws` for `yksvadur.ja@gmail.com`.
- Work Google auth should stay isolated in `~/.config/gws-xvadur` for `adam@xvadur.com`.
- Work account authentication was successfully completed and verified for Gmail and Calendar access.

## Agent workspace constraints

- Telegram agents use separate per-agent workspaces under `/Users/_xvadur/singularitas/agents/{cso,cro,cmo,coo}`.
- `goplaces` works in the runtime, but per-agent Telegram workspaces cannot invoke it until the skill is installed/synced into those workspaces.
