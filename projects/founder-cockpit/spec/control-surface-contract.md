# Founder Control Surface Contract

## Required sections

1. `Approvals`
2. `Leads needing action today`
3. `Callbacks at risk`
4. `Deployment blocked`
5. `Proof/content ready`
6. `Web maintenance queue`
7. `Single best next move`

## Section rules

- every section must cite a source
- if a source is unavailable, print `NO_SIGNAL` and point to the missing system
- no section may be silently dropped
- final ordering is founder-first, not source-system-first
